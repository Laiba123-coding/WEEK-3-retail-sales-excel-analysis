# 💡 Key Insights (Python Analysis)

- Air is the most frequently used shipment mode, accounting for approximately **63%** of all shipments.
- South Africa recorded the highest shipment volume.
- Deliveries are completed approximately **6 days earlier** than the scheduled delivery date.
- Trimble has the highest total freight cost among all vendors.
- Freight costs are concentrated among a small number of vendors.

---

# 📊 Power BI Dashboard Insights

- Air transport dominates the shipment network.
- South Africa contributes the highest shipment volume.
- Overall delivery performance is ahead of schedule, indicating efficient logistics operations.

---

# 💼 Business Recommendations

### 1️⃣ Optimize Vendor Costs

Review contracts with high-cost vendors such as **Trimble** to identify opportunities for freight cost optimization.

### 2️⃣ Evaluate Shipment Modes

Since air shipments account for the majority of deliveries, evaluate whether some shipments can be shifted to lower-cost transportation methods without affecting delivery performance.
